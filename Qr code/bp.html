🌸 Bauhinia Purpurea (గులాబి దేవగాజ్జె) 🌸

The Bauhinia purpurea, commonly called the Orchid Tree or Purple Bauhinia, is a stunning flowering tree known for its butterfly-shaped leaves and vibrant purple-pink blossoms. This fast-growing tree adds spectacular color to landscapes across tropical and subtropical regions.

📊 Classification:

Belonging to the Fabaceae family, this deciduous tree grows 6-10 meters tall. Its unique twin-lobed leaves resemble camel hoof prints or butterfly wings, making it easily identifiable.

💜✨ Interesting Facts:

  • Butterfly Leaves: The bilobed leaves fold up at night like butterfly wings 🦋
  • Medicinal Power: Used in Ayurveda for treating diabetes, wounds and infections 💊
  • Pollinator Magnet: Attracts bees, butterflies and sunbirds with its nectar-rich flowers 🐝
  • Rapid Grower: Can grow 2-3 feet per year under ideal conditions 📈

🇮🇳 Indian Connection:

  • Urban Beauty: Adorns gardens in Hyderabad and Bengaluru with purple blooms 🌆
  • Temple Flower: Used in South Indian temple decorations and rituals 🛕
  • Monsoon Bloomer: Flowers spectacularly after the first monsoon rains 🌧️
  • Street Elegance: Planted along roads in Pune and Mumbai for shade and beauty 🛣️

🌱 Growth Guide:

  • Sunlight: Thrives in full sun to partial shade ☀️
  • Soil: Prefers well-drained loamy soil 🌿
  • Watering: Moderate watering, drought-tolerant once established 💧
  • Pruning: Prune after flowering to maintain shape ✂️
